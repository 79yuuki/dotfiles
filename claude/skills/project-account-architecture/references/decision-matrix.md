# Split vs Merge Decision Matrix

## Split when
- different buyer / user audience
- separate product narrative
- enough weekly content volume
- dedicated owner exists
- risk of mixed messaging is high

## Keep merged when
- founder distribution is the only real channel
- low posting cadence
- overlapping audience and CTA
- no dedicated owner

## Required outputs
- account name
- audience
- purpose
- content lanes
- CTA
- owner
- cadence target
